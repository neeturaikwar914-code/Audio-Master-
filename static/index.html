    How to Use

    1. Tap or drag your audio file into the upload area.
    2. Adjust FX sliders: Reverb, Pitch Shift, EQ.
    3. Click "Start Processing" to generate stems.
    4. Preview your audio using the waveform player.
    5. Download the processed file once ready.

    FX Guide

    Flat EQ: Neutral sound, no boost.
    Pop EQ: Boosts vocals and midrange.
    Rock EQ: Boosts guitar & drums.
    Acoustic EQ: Smooth, natural tone.
    Bass Boost: Heavy low-end emphasis.

    FAQ

    Q: How long does processing take?
    A: Depends on file size and FX, usually 10–30 seconds per song.
    Q: Which formats are supported?
    A: MP3, WAV, FLAC, AAC, M4A.
    Q: Can I batch process multiple files?
    A: Yes, the batch upload section handles multiple files.
    Q: Is this mobile-friendly?
    A: Yes, all UI is optimized for touch and small screens.
    Q: How do I adjust FX while processing?
    A: Use the sliders before hitting "Start Processing".
    Q: Can I preview before download?
    A: Yes, use the waveform preview player.
